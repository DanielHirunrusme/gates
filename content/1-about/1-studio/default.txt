#Who We Are

Gates is a team of creatives who live to tell a brand story. Big stories. Small stories. True stories. Stories that connect people with brands. We have an instinct and the experience for knowing what customers want, not just today, but tomorrow.

Gates was founded in 2014 by Cecilia Gates. Cecilia has 15+ years experience leading and developing in-house creative teams for global retail brands such as GAP, SEPHORA, LOFT and most recently launching Lou & Grey for Ann Inc.

#What We Do

We evolve your brands into the new world of retail. We work with you to establish a strong brand presence. 

#How We Do it

We believe you are the experts on you — we just help you see your brand differently.
